Vision, Mission, and CORE Values

VISION

FEU Alabang aims to be a globally-recognized educational institution fostering excellence through innovation, research, and linkages that will best serve the nation and the world.

MISSION

FEU Alabang is committed to providing quality student-centered education anchored on the changing needs and demands of the global industry and society. It is dedicated to producing globally competitive, socially responsive, and morally upright graduates who are leaders of innovation and change through transformative and multi-diverse learning experiences.

CORE VALUES

Fortitude

Excellence
